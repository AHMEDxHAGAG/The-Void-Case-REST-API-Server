# THE ATLANTIC WOLFFISH CASE
**Setting:** A moving train
**Victim:** The Swimming Victim
**The Blackened:** The Brawler

---

## Overview

On the day of the deadline, a murder takes place in the spa and sauna car of a moving train. The Swimming Victim is found dead in the pool, having been stabbed three times in the abdomen. Her body is discovered floating in bloodied water with the pool cover activated over her.

Two witnesses — The Documenter and his companion The Shadow — stumble onto the killer mid-act and are violently attacked to silence them. The killer then changes into fresh clothes, disposes of the evidence through a convenient laundry service, and is the one to report the body — framing herself as a concerned bystander.

The killer is The Brawler.

---

## Timeline

**~2:00 PM**
The Documenter and The Shadow begin setting up cameras around the train, taking photos of themselves at each placement as timestamps. They set cameras in the following order: the sleeper car hallway, the spa and sauna, the kitchen, and the dining car.

After placing all the cameras, the two take a food break — grabbing food from the kitchen and sitting in the dining car to eat. The Documenter reminds The Shadow about a prior arrangement involving hair care, and both return to The Documenter's cabin to collect products and swimwear.

**~2:40 PM**
On their way back through the hallway, the two pass The Swimming Victim leaving her cabin for the first time in a long time. A simple greeting is exchanged.

**~2:45 PM**
The Brawler leaves her cabin to find food. She passes through the hallway after the hallway camera has already taken its 2:40 PM photo — the camera captures The Swimming Victim and the others, but not The Brawler. She heads to the kitchen.

In the sauna car, she notices The Swimming Victim already there. She recognizes an opportunity.

**~2:47 PM**
The Brawler is in the kitchen. Knowing the knife cabinet is locked, she uses a hoof knife from her own possessions to cut the camera straps that had been looped around the cabinet handles as an improvised lock. She takes a kitchen knife as her murder weapon. She notices a camera has been set on the counter — she flips it to face a wall before the next shutter goes off.

She returns to the sauna with the knife.

**~2:45–3:00 PM**
The Brawler stabs The Swimming Victim three times in the abdomen. She drags the body to the pool and drops it in, then activates the pool cover to conceal it.

**~3:00 PM**
The Documenter and The Shadow arrive at the sauna. They find The Brawler mid-act — still there as they enter. The Brawler panics. She shoves The Documenter out of his wheelchair and attacks The Shadow first, throwing the wheelchair at him to knock him out. She then turns on The Documenter, who is on the floor trying to defend himself. She stabs his hand when he raises it to shield himself, then kicks and stomps on his face until he loses consciousness — or something close to it.

**~3:00–3:30 PM**
The Brawler goes back to her cabin and changes into a completely clean outfit and new shoes, disposing of her bloodied clothes via the train's laundry service. The service discards soiled clothes entirely rather than washing them — meaning the bloody evidence is gone. She makes sure she is spotless.

**~3:30 PM**
The Brawler exits her cabin and reports the body.

## Key Contradictions

1. **Sauna Camera [2:45 PM] vs. The Brawler's Account** — The Brawler claimed she only passed by the sauna on her way back from the kitchen and saw The Documenter and The Shadow looking at the body. The camera shows her and The Swimming Victim entering the sauna together at 2:45 PM — before The Documenter and The Shadow arrived.

2. **Kitchen Camera [2:47 PM] — The Wall** — The camera was flipped to face a wall. The Documenter and The Shadow had no reason to do this. Someone didn't want the next photo to show them in the kitchen.

3. **Hoof Knife in the Trash** — The Brawler used a hoof knife from her own possessions to cut the camera straps and access the knife cabinet. The Documenter has his own box of sharp tools and would never need one. The hoof knife belongs to The Brawler.

4. **Laundry Service Logs** — The train's laundry service disposes of clothes entirely rather than washing them. The Brawler was the last person to use the service. Her original outfit — which would have been covered in blood — is gone.

5. **The Documenter's Shoe Print** — The bruise and shoe print on The Documenter's face. Whoever kicked and stomped on him left a print. The Brawler attacked him directly.

---

## The Correct Accusation

**The Brawler.**

She was captured entering the sauna with The Swimming Victim at 2:45 PM by the sauna camera — directly contradicting her claim to have only passed by later. She flipped the kitchen camera before it could photograph her cutting the cabinet straps and taking the knife. She used a hoof knife from her own possessions rather than any tool available to the others. She destroyed the evidence by using the laundry service immediately after the attack. She reported the body herself.
