# THE VOID CASE
**Setting:** An underground facility
**Trial victims:** The Stabbing Victim, The Strangled Victim, The Sawn Victim, The Stabbed Floor Victim
**The Blackened:** The Scheduler

---

## Overview

Four people are killed across several locations in the underground facility. The investigation begins after a pre-recorded broadcast — played from the TV station by a masked, cloaked figure with a voice changer — instructs the group to search specific areas. When they finally unlock the Void of Judgement gates, a fifth victim dies instantly as a rigged saw falls on them. The trial begins immediately after.

The killer is The Scheduler: methodical, present all night, and the only person with access to every location and the knowledge to build every trap.

---

## Timeline

**~8:00 PM**
The Scheduler purchases a costume, a second phone, and a voice changer from the mall. She visits The Observer while disguised and asks for her help. The Observer refuses to participate in the plan but reveals where The Operator is being held — in the basement of a nearby residence, with others present.

**~8:30 PM**
The Scheduler buys knockout gas from the mall. It is only sold in packages of exactly three bottles — no more, no less. She throws one bottle into the basement, knocking out everyone inside, then unties The Operator and drags them out. She asks The Operator about the Void of Judgement gate key and about any secrets in the Mirror Labyrinth. The Operator, coerced, cooperates.

Using the second phone, The Scheduler texts her planned victims to go to specific locations at specific times. The Stabbing Victim (The Courier's companion, referred to here as The Floor Victim) replies that he'll be coming with someone else — The Witness — so The Scheduler must account for that. She also texts The Drifter to come to the Mirror Labyrinth at midnight.

**~9:00 PM**
The Scheduler lures The Stabbing Victim to the Planetarium Lounge area. She stabs him multiple times — wounds both on his back and front. She brings one of the space suits from the Planetarium Lounge, stuffs his body into it, then returns the suit to the display stand. The suit is still wet on the outside when found, indicating recent use.

**~9:30 PM**
The Scheduler uses the second knockout gas bottle on The Strangled Victim in her own room. She strangles her to death, stuffs her body into the second astronaut suit, and places her inside the Zero Gravity Chamber — activating it to simulate an astronaut floating in space.

**~10:00 PM**
The Scheduler prepares the Mirror Labyrinth trap for The Floor Victim. Using information from The Operator about the labyrinth's secret lower level, she places random objects on the ground to mark a path — the trick to navigating the maze's disorienting effect. She locates the trapdoor to the maze below and replaces a floor panel with a one-way mirror panel: reflective from above, transparent from below. She drills a hole through it — just wide enough for a knife blade.

Via text, she instructs The Floor Victim to come to the labyrinth and to crouch at a specific marked panel. When he does, The Scheduler stabs him from below through the drilled hole — using the same knife from the earlier stabbing. The Witness, distracted and confused, catches a glimpse of what may be The Operator nearby (either in person or as a reflection). Before he can investigate, The Floor Victim is already bleeding on the ground. The two panic and leave.

**~11:00 PM**
The Scheduler lures The Sawn Victim to the locker rooms and uses the third and final knockout gas bottle on him. With him unconscious, she carries him to the Void of Judgement gates and binds him to a mirror panel with ropes — just in case he regains consciousness. He is not dead yet. The rigged saw is set above him, connected to the gate mechanism: when the gates are unlocked and opened, the saw falls.

**~11:50 PM – 12:00 AM**
The Scheduler meets The Drifter at the Mirror Labyrinth as arranged by text. She pushes The Drifter down through the trapdoor into the lower maze, then replaces the panel above, sealing her in. She leaves the second phone — loaded with all the incriminating texts she sent — in the lower maze to frame The Drifter.

**~12:30 AM**
Much earlier, The Scheduler sent an anonymous letter to the TV station hosts telling them to broadcast from the plaza instead of the studio (citing the disappearance of the facility's authority figure). With the studio empty, she enters in her costume and mask, and plays the pre-recorded video she made. The video — broadcast through the regular channel, appearing live — instructs the group in a modified voice to investigate the Planetarium Lounge, the Mirror Labyrinth, and the Void of Judgement.

**After the investigation**
The group investigates all locations, collecting evidence. When they finally unlock the Void of Judgement gates, the rigged saw falls — killing The Sawn Victim instantly. There is no time to examine the scene. The trial begins immediately.

## Locations and Key Evidence

### Basement
- **Knockout Gas Bottle #1** — Used and shattered. One of three from the only pack sold.
- **Cut Ropes** — The ropes that held The Operator captive, cut on the ground.

### Planetarium Lounge
- **Stabbing Victim's Body** — Multiple thin, deep stab wounds on both back and front. Stuffed into a space suit after death. A lot of blood beneath the suit.
- **Wet Space Suit** — The suit the victim was found in is wet on the outside, indicating recent use.
- **Missing Suit Stand** — One display stand is empty. A suit is gone.
- **Work Notice** — Most employees were out of their usual locations for preparations, meaning fewer witnesses.

### Mirror Labyrinth
- **Object Trail** — Random small objects placed in a deliberate path from the entrance to a specific floor panel circled by objects.
- **One-Way Panel** — The circled panel is a one-way mirror with a knife-sized hole drilled through it. Heavy blood on top. Blood and shoe prints across the floor in multiple directions.
- **Lower Maze** — A second mirror maze below, accessible by removing the one-way panel. The Drifter is found inside, unable to get out.
- **Framing Phone** — A new phone with no owner in the group. Fresh number. Full of texts used to orchestrate the killings. Left deliberately.
- **Saw Drill** — A battery-powered electric saw drill found in the lower maze. No outlet needed.
- **Knife** — The knife used for both stabbings, with a long bloodied blade.

### Zero Gravity Chamber
- **Strangulation Victim's Body** — No injuries except rope marks on her neck. Stuffed into a space suit and arranged in the chamber with it running, positioned to appear as a floating astronaut.

### Void of Judgement
- **The Sawn Victim's Body** — Bound to a mirror panel by ropes near the gate. The metal saw fell directly on him when the gates opened, killing him from severe blood loss.
- **Binding Ropes** — Still attached to the panel.
- **Rigged Saw** — Battery-powered, no outlet required. Set to fall when the gate opened.
- **Void Panel** — The same type of one-way mirror panel as in the labyrinth.

### TV Station
- **Broadcast Recording** — Pre-recorded video of a cloaked, masked figure with a modified voice, directing the group to search specific locations. Made to appear live.
- **Flash Drive** — Contains the recorded video. Left in the studio.
- **Anonymous Letter** — Sent to the hosts earlier, instructing them to film in the plaza and leave the studio free. No name.

### Mall
- **Knockout Gas Info** — Only sold in packages of three. Always three.

### Locker Rooms
- **Knockout Gas Bottle #2** — Used and shattered.

### Gatekeeper's Room
- **Knockout Gas Bottle #3** — Used and shattered.

## Key Contradictions

1. **Wet Space Suit + Stabbing Victim's Body** — The suit was used and returned to display. The victim was placed in it after death. Two suits used, one stand empty. This was prepared in advance.
2. **Object Trail + One-Way Panel** — The objects guided someone to a specific panel on purpose. The panel had a drilled knife-hole. This was a guided assassination, not a trap.
3. **One-Way Panel + Void Panel** — Same panel type in two separate locations. One for the stabbing, one for the saw. The same person built both setups.
4. **Broadcast Recording + Anonymous Letter** — The letter cleared the studio. The broadcast played from that same empty studio. The letter writer and the broadcaster are the same person.
5. **Knockout Gas Bottle #1 + Knockout Gas Info** — Sold in packs of three, always three. Three victims incapacitated, three bottles found. One buyer.
6. **Framing Phone** — New phone, new number, full of texts. Belongs to no one in the group. Left deliberately to be found.

---

## The Correct Accusation

**The Scheduler.**

She had the costume and voice changer to remain anonymous. She had The Operator's knowledge of the labyrinth and the gate. She had access to every location. She cleared the TV studio with the anonymous letter and used it herself. The knockout gas came in threes — three victims, three bottles, one buyer. The panel type connects the labyrinth and the void. The phone in the lower maze was not The Drifter's. It was planted.
